WYRDSCAPE

A 3D browser MMORPG of the Wyrd Lands

How to play:

  • Left-click the ground to walk there
  • Left-click trees, rocks, monsters, NPCs to interact
  • Right-click objects for a context menu of actions
  • Arrow Left / Right rotate the camera
  • Arrow Up / Down tilt the camera
  • + / - zoom in and out
  • Tabs on the right show Stats, Inventory, and Skills
  • Game auto-saves to your browser every 10 seconds

Vertical slice — pure procedural 3D, no downloads, no install.
